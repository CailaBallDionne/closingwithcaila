About the Author

Caila Ball-Dionne

Licensed CA Real Estate Agent  |  CA DRE #02439602  |  The Borges Real Estate Team at eXp

I'm a licensed real estate agent based in La Crescenta, serving the Foothills, La Cañada Flintridge, Northeast LA, and beyond. Before real estate, I spent more than 20 years leading marketing strategy for agencies and brands, work that taught me how to reach the right audience, tell a compelling story, and execute under pressure.

I write about real estate the way I practice it: plainly, honestly, and with real respect for how much is at stake in a home transaction. Every article on this site is written from experience, not filler.

I'm part of The Borges Real Estate Team at eXp, and I bring the full resources of that team to every client I work with.
